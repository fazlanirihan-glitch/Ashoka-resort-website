ATTRACTIONS

Thrilling Rides & Activities

From heart-pounding water slides to kid-friendly splash zones, every moment at Ashoka is packed with excitement and adventure.

Thrill

High Velocity Water Slides

Race down our towering multi-lane water slides at breakneck speed. Feel the rush as gravity pulls you through twists and turns before splashing into the pool below.

Adventure

Spiral Tube Slides

Navigate through enclosed spiral tubes with unexpected twists and turns. These colorful tube slides offer an exhilarating journey from top to bottom.

Fun

Rain Dance Arena

Dance under cascading waterfalls with DJ music, LED lights, and fog effects. Our rain dance zone is the ultimate party experience for all ages.

Kids

Kids Water Zone

A safe and colorful splash area designed exclusively for little ones. Features mini slides, mushroom showers, water cannons, and shallow pools.

Relax

Main Swimming Pool

Crystal-clear water in our expansive main pool, perfect for leisurely swims. Professional lifeguards ensure safety while you float and relax.

Nature

Beach Access

Walk directly from the resort to the golden sands of Harnai Beach. Enjoy stunning sunsets, beachside walks, and the soothing sounds of ocean waves.

EXPLORE DAPOLI

Nearby Attractions in Dapoli

Dapoli is a treasure trove of natural beauty and historical landmarks. Explore these popular destinations just a short drive from Ashoka Resort.

Harnai Beach

A serene and scenic beach known for its golden sands, vibrant fishing harbor, and breathtaking sunsets along the Konkan coast.

2 km away

Suvarnadurg Fort

A majestic sea fort built by the Marathas, accessible by boat from Harnai Port. Explore the ancient ramparts and enjoy panoramic ocean views.

5 km away

Ladghar Beach

Famous for its unique reddish-brown sand cliffs and pristine shoreline, Ladghar Beach is a hidden gem of the Konkan coastline.

12 km away

Murud Beach

A tranquil and less-crowded beach ideal for swimming and water sports. Enjoy the clean sands and gentle waves in a peaceful setting.

15 km away
