Experience the Thrill

From heart-pounding water slides to relaxing pools and beach access, Ashoka Water Park has something for every age and adventure level.

Thrill

High Velocity Water Slides

Race down our fastest slides with near-vertical drops and high-speed twists that will leave you breathless

Adventure

Spiral Tube Slides

Navigate enclosed spiral tubes with surprise twists and turns, ending in massive splash pools

Party

Rain Dance Arena

Dance under cascading water jets with colorful LED lights and pumping DJ music

Fun

Water Splash Buckets

Giant tipping buckets that drench everyone below — a refreshing surprise every few minutes

Relax

Mushroom Waterfalls

Stand under gentle mushroom-shaped cascading waterfalls for a soothing, fun experience

Family

Kids Water Zone

Safe, shallow-water play area designed exclusively for children with mini slides and interactive features

Swim

Main Swimming Pool

Large crystal-clear pool with designated zones for beginners, intermediate, and experienced swimmers

Kids

Toy Train Ride

A charming train ride through the resort for the little ones to enjoy a scenic tour

Nature

Beach Access

Step directly onto Harnai Beach from the resort for swimming, sunbathing, and scenic walks
